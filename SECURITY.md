## Reporting a Vulnerability

To report a vulnerability, please follow these steps:

1. Go to **Issues** tab.
2. Click **Create new issue**
3. Add tag **Vulnerability**
4. Describe your problem
5. Confirm

**Congratulations!** Your issue ready.
